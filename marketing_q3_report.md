# Marketing Performance Report — Q3

## Campaign Spend Summary

Total digital campaign spend in Q3 was $320K, distributed as follows:

| Channel           | Spend   | Leads | CPL    |
|-------------------|---------|-------|--------|
| Paid Search (SEM) | $192K   | 1,840 | $104   |
| LinkedIn Ads      | $80K    | 420   | $190   |
| Display / Retarget| $32K    | 280   | $114   |
| Content syndication| $16K   | 95    | $168   |
| **Total**         | **$320K** | **2,635** | **$121** |

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

CAC for Q3 was $1,240, down from $1,450 in Q2. The improvement reflects better lead quality from paid search optimisation and a new qualification process implemented by the SDR team.

## Pipeline Generated

Q3 campaigns generated $4.8M in pipeline, of which $1.1M has already closed. The sales cycle average is 47 days.

## Q4 Budget

The approved Q4 marketing budget is $380K — a 19% increase over Q3, funded by the strong Q3 revenue performance. The incremental budget will be allocated to:
- LinkedIn sponsored content (+$30K)
- Event sponsorships (+$20K)
- SEO content production (+$10K)

## Top Performing Campaigns

1. "Scale Without the Complexity" SEM campaign — 38% conversion rate on landing page
2. LinkedIn CEO thought leadership series — generated 12 enterprise demos
3. G2 review campaign — lifted review count from 45 to 78, boosting organic traffic 22%
