# HR Policy Manual

## Parental Leave Policy

All employees are entitled to 16 weeks of fully paid parental leave, applicable to both primary and secondary caregivers. Leave must be taken within 12 months of the child's birth or adoption.

## Annual Leave

Full-time employees receive 24 days of paid annual leave per year, accrued at 2 days per month. Unused leave may be carried over up to a maximum of 10 days.

## Remote Work Policy

Employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Full remote arrangements require VP approval and are reviewed quarterly. All remote employees must maintain core hours of 10am–3pm in their local timezone.

## Expense Claims

Expense claims must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date via the HR portal with receipts attached. Claims over $500 require manager approval. International travel expenses require VP approval before travel.

## Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted twice a year — in June and December. Employees receive written feedback and a rating on a 5-point scale. Ratings of 4 or above qualify for the annual bonus pool.

## Medical Benefits

All full-time employees receive comprehensive medical insurance covering the employee and up to 4 dependants. The company covers 90% of premiums. Dental and vision are included.

## Total Headcount

As of Q3, total headcount is 142 employees across 6 departments, with 28 open positions currently being recruited for.
