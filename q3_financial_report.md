# Q3 Financial Report

## Executive Summary

Q3 was a strong quarter. Net revenue reached $4.2M, representing a 12% year-over-year increase driven by new enterprise contracts signed in July and August.

## Revenue Breakdown

| Revenue Stream        | Q3 Amount | Q2 Amount | Change  |
|-----------------------|-----------|-----------|---------|
| SaaS subscriptions    | $3.1M     | $2.8M     | +10.7%  |
| Professional services | $0.8M     | $0.7M     | +14.3%  |
| Marketplace fees      | $0.3M     | $0.2M     | +50.0%  |
| **Total**             | **$4.2M** | **$3.7M** | **+13.5%** |

## Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)

ARR stands at $18.5M as of end of Q3, up from $14.2M at the same point last year — a 30% YoY increase.

## Expenses

Total operating expenses in Q3 were $3.6M, up from $3.2M in Q2. The increase is primarily due to 8 new hires in engineering and sales.

## Cash Position

The company holds $14.4M in cash and equivalents. Monthly burn rate is $800K. This gives a cash runway of approximately 18 months at current burn.

## Gross Margin

Gross margin improved to 74% in Q3, up from 71% in Q2, reflecting improved infrastructure cost efficiency.

## Forecast

Q4 revenue is projected at $4.6–4.9M based on the current pipeline. Three enterprise deals totalling $1.2M ARR are expected to close in Q4.
